I finished my master at UCLA ECE department, working with Prof. Vwani Roychowdhury. I work closely with Prof. Jianguo Zhang and Dr. Hongwei (Bran) Li on medical imaging analysis during my master and my bachelor.

    Learning Brain Tumor Representation in 3D High-Resolution MR Images via Interpretable State Space Models
    In this project, we investigate the State Space Model, especially Mamba for radiomics analysis in terms of downstream classification task and its latent space interpretability.
    Qingqiao Hu, Daoan Zhang, Jiebo Luo, Zhenyu Gong, Benedikt Wiestler, Jianguo Zhang, Hongwei Bran Li

    Bayeisan One-Encoder-Multiple-Decoder (BOEMD) Network
    In this project, we1 proposed a simple yet effective Bayesian Neural Network (BNN) framework, especifically designed for inter-rater uncertainty quantification probelm in medical imaging. We call this framework, Bayesian One-Encoder-Multiple-Decoder (BOEMD) network.
    Qingqiao Hu, Hao Wang*, Jing Luo, Yunhao Luo, Zhiheng Zhangg, Jan S. Kirschke, Benedikt Wiestler, Bjoern Menze, Jianguo Zhang, Hongwei Bran Li
