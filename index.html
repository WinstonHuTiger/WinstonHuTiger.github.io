    Biography

    Hi, I am Qingqiao (Winston) Hu, an incoming CS PhD student at Stony Brook University. I finished my master at UCLA ECE department, worked in Prof. Vwani Roychowdhury’s Complex Networks Lab on tooth segmentation for smart tooth brush. Meanwhile, I work very closely with Prof. Jianguo Zhang and Dr. Hongwei (Bran) Li in medical imaging. I obtained my bachelor degree of engineering in Computer Science and Technology at SUSTech under the supervision of Prof. Jianguo Zhang.
